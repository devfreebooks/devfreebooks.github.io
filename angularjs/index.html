AngularJS

AngularJS is a toolset for building the framework most suited to your application development. It is fully extensible and works well with other libraries. Every feature can be modified or replaced to suit your unique development workflow and feature needs.

  • Angular Testing Succinctly

    Though sometimes perceived as a hurdle, testing can save developers from heartbreak in the long run. In Angular Testing Succinctly, author Joseph D. Booth provides a quick introduction to different tools and approaches to testing Angular projects to make them higher quality from the start and easier to maintain down the road. Topics covered include lint checking, test-driven development, unit testing, and end-to-end testing. Tools covered include TSLint, ESLint, SonarLint, Karma, Jasmine, Protractor, and Cucumber.

    Author: Joseph D. Booth
    Pages: 94 | Year: 2020 | Language: EN
    Added: 2021-10-15

    Angular 2 is a massively popular JavaScript framework built to take advantage of component development in web apps. In Angular 2 Succinctly, author Joseph D. Booth will guide you through setting up a development environment, interacting with the Angular CLI, building Hello World, and more.
